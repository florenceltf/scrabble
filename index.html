Scrabble Calculator


A tool to accompany your game of Scrabble.

Instructions
  1. Type your word into the text field and your score will update as you type.
  2. Select any applicable bonus options.
  3. Playing all seven tiles will activate 'bingo', adding 50 points to your word score.
  4. To reference a blank tile, use an asterisks "*" in place of a letter.
  5. Press Enter or click Submit Word to make your word appear on the word list.
  6. Click Reset to clear the page.
